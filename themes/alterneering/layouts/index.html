Alterneering Studios is a speculative design studio that engineers alternatives by bringing them to life. We do this by creating realities with their own logic, norms, characters and stories. Within these, we design user-led artefacts with the tools and technologies available to us today. With these artefacts, we aim to create moments of doubt to influence the perception of existing products, services and systems.

Founded by Nicole Stoll, Luca Milan and Miles Mayr, we combine our different backgrounds in the field of human-centred design to explore future possibilities.
